Tsunami Inundation Mitigation

Project Explanation

For my final project, I was tasked with identifying a community that is threatened by a natural hazard and plan a response to mitigate the impact. I decided to choose my hometown of Lincoln City, OR as the Cascadia Subduction Zone off the coastline threatens an earthquake with a subsequent tsunami. My focus was on the tsunami hazard specifically.

The map shows the result of one of my policy objectives to mitigate the tsunami hazard. It depicts the introduction of a new critical facility to provide aid after a tsunami hits. The purple polygons depict current critical facility coverage while the green shows additional coverage. The dots depict inhabited land parcels. The additional critical facility provides more coverage to uncovered residents.
