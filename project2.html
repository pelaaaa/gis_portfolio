Coastal Vulnerability Assessment

Project Explanation

This class was centered on using ArcGIS Pro to conduct spatial analyses. The final project was broad; my only requirement was to identify a spatial problem and plan a response for it. I decided to conduct a coastal vulnerability assessment to identify sections of the Oregon coastline most vulnerable to sea level rise.

As climate change continues, the Oregon coastline will be threatened by additional, exacerbated environmental stressors. One of these is sea level rise. The resulting index shows sections of the coastline that are most vulnerable to sea level rise.
